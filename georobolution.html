How to play GeoRoboLution
🎯
What is GeoRoboLution?

GeoRoboLution is a maths adventure where you build a robot by solving questions! You have 30 minutes to complete all 3 levels.

Each level uses the GeomTech method — you must show your working before entering your answer!

📐
The GeomTech method

GeomTech uses shapes to multiply. For 2 × 3: draw a shape with 3 corners (matching the second number). Place 2 dots at each corner (the first number). Count all the dots — that is your answer!

In every level, you will see a GeomTech working box. Fill in the corner values first, then enter your final answer.

🏆
The 3 levels
⚡
Level 1 — Multiply with GeomTech
7 multiplication questions using shapes
🧩
Level 2 — Problem Solving
3 word problems — find the multiplication hidden inside
🚀
Level 3 — Think Deeper (HOTS)
3 higher-order questions — show your reasoning
🤖
Build your robot

Every correct answer earns a robot part. The home screen shows your robot with silhouettes for parts not yet earned.

Level 1 → Head, antenna, eyes
Level 2 → Body, arms, hands
Level 3 → Legs, feet, power core
⏱
Timer & password

You have 30 minutes to complete the game. The timer is shown at the top. When it turns red, you are running low on time!

If the timer runs out, you will need to enter a password from your teacher to continue.
